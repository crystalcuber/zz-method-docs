On July 27, 2025, Yoruba achieved a 6.51 ZZ World Record ao5 at [Dragon Cubing X 2025](https://www.worldcubeassociation.org/competitions/DragonCubingX2025).
He smashed his [6.93 ZZ WR](/en/example-solves/yoruba-6.93-ao5) from less than 2 months ago.
This average was ranked 7th in Poland, 50th in Europe and 193rd in the world.
